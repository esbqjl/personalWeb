About Me：

I'm Wenjun Zhang, an academically accomplished computer science and electrical engineering professional currently pursuing a Master of Science at Boston University. I'm proficient in English and native in Chinese and Cantonese. My interests include reading, basketball, badminton, and computer games. My passion for computer sciences and electrical engineering compels me to innovate and strive for excellence. I believe in the transformative power of technology and am eager to contribute to it.
